# Post Ship Survey — product spec (benchmark input for PR #18835)

NOTE: the 3-month cooldown / "no survey in any subsequent month" / reset clauses have been
removed from the live spec — do NOT test cooldown or repeat-suppression behavior.

## Summary
Automated, title-specific one-question survey delivered via a website pop-up immediately after
members ship their box, replacing the manual email workflow. The pop-up asks one of two
attribution questions to a monthly randomized sample.

## Website + App experience
A website pop-up for each question that specifies the title of the main selection the member
chose as their BOTM that month:
- "Did you first hear about [title] from BOTM?" — a) Yes  b) No
- "If BOTM had not featured [title], how likely is it that you would have purchased it elsewhere?"
  — a) Very unlikely  b) Somewhat unlikely  c) Neither  d) Somewhat likely  e) Very likely

The pop-up occurs right after a member ships their box.

## Audience
- Each pop-up goes to a random sample of members who shipped a box; two questions, no overlap —
  any qualifying member receives only ONE of the questions in a given month.
- **Exclude any member who is becoming a Friend or BFF that month** (they already receive the New
  Loyalty Tier popup flow). The exclusion is scoped to the month they become Friend/BFF.

## Implementation details
- Surveys are under feature flag ID: 135.
- Survey question + answers editable in admin (ID 50 + ID 51).
- Yes/No surveys and scale surveys both supported in core; %Title% substitution injects the
  member's current BOTM main selection title into the question.
- ~1.5% of members shipping a box each see one of the two surveys (≈3% total).
- Members becoming Friends/BFFs do NOT see the survey in the month they become Friends/BFFs.
- account_message tracks whether a member dismissed the survey.
